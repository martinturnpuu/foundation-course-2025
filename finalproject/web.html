Our Story

Founded in 2003 as an independent store by a group of friends, we made it our mission to keep the vinyl experience alive for generations to come. We care deeply not only about the quality of our products but also about preserving the legacy of creators and musicians who have made their mark in music history.

Our Product

Trusted by award-winning artists and music enthusiasts worldwide. Our facilities produce over 100,000 premium vinyl records yearly, distributed through 100+ specialty shops across the globe. Each record is crafted with precision and passion for the authentic vinyl experience.
